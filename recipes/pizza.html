Pizza


In this image you can see a homemade delicious pizza of pepperonni ready to be eaten

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperonni

Steps

  1. First you get the dough
  2. Then you spread the dough over the table to make it the form you want
  3. Then you spread the tomato sauce over the dough
  4. Then you place the gratinated cheesse over your tomato sauce
  5. And place some pepperoni slices over the cheesse
  6. And then you place it on the oven for 15 minutes
